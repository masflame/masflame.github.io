About Spark

Spark is a mobile-first platform offering practical, job-ready training through micro-courses powered by AI and AR. We make skill acquisition accessible and engaging, equipping South African youth for employment.

Key Features

AI-Powered Micro-Courses

Fast, targeted training in high-demand areas, personalized for each learner.

3D/AR Simulations

Realistic simulations for hands-on practice, giving learners practical experience.

Accessible Anytime

Mobile-first design to reach youth in remote areas with affordable courses.

Multi-Language Support

Courses available in Setswana, Afrikaans, and more, fostering inclusivity.
